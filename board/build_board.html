THE CONCEPT

1940s Body, 2022 Capability

This build starts with a 2022 Ram 2500 Power Wagon Crew Cab and reimagines it as a 1940s flat-fender Power Wagon, while keeping the modern Ram chassis, drivetrain, and four-wheel-drive capability underneath. It looks like the truck the GIs brought home and drives like a current heavy-duty 4×4.

Two and a half years. Tens of thousands of hours.
